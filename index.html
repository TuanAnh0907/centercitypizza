Center City Pizza was started in 2010 with a "Good Food Fast" menu concept and specializes in many varieties of hand tossed extra large neopolitan, deep dish, and sicilian style pizza baked in our state of the art brick-lined stone hearth ovens. Dough is made from scratch daily and our signature sauce is made from vined-ripened San Marzano Italian tomatoes.

Conveniently located in the Food Court in the Upper Level of the Center City Mall with ample garage parking, we have comfortable seating for over a hundred people and offer a mezzanine level dining room which can be reserved for private parties and luncheons. Open air contemporary design, large flat panel TV's , complementary Wi Fi service, and spacious and comfortable dining areas make our facility one of the most unique pizzeria restaurants in the area.
